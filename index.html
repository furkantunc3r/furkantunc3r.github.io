My Story

As a former English teacher with a fervent passion for technology, my journey has been defined by a passionate transition into the world of coding at a unique, teacherless self-learning school named 42. This decision was a pivotal moment in my life.

I am fueled by my unwavering passion, enthusiasm, and an insatiable curiosity for all things related to computers and software. The process of acquiring new skills and constantly pushing myself to improve is what drives me. I never shy away from challenges; instead, I embrace them as opportunities to learn and grow.

With an analytical mindset and a strong problem-solving acumen, I am naturally inclined to find innovative solutions to complex issues. I take pride in my ability to bring a valuable skill set to any company, ensuring that I consistently deliver quality code and meaningful accomplishments. I am committed to continuous learning and look forward to engaging with companies that share this value and offer opportunities where I can contribute my skills and enthusiasm to make a difference.
